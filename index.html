Mike is a designer from Los Angeles. Currently designing digital products at Webhouse in Czech Republic.

I cover the spectrum of the design process, from the early stages of Information Architecture and Wireframes to Prototyping and Final Design Approval. Empathy plays a role in my design process. Being an Enneagram Type 9, I go into every project with a focus on understanding people’s needs with the product design. Which helps inform my design decisions.

Fun facts: Outside of design, I enjoy traveling and experiencing new cultures and foods. I love talking camera gear, tech and football (soccer). I have a thing for finding and supporting new up and coming bands no one has heard of yet. I also curate playlists. Have a listen!
